"Owned and operated by Derek and Alex Bray, Slope & Hatch has been serving artisinal tacos, hot dogs, and all manner of street food out of their downtown Glenwood Springs location since late 2014. The restaurant has flourished in the competitive Roaring Fork Valley through their creative specials and diverse menu items that range from Thai curry vegetarian tacos to midwestern coney dogs and Carolina style BBQ pork nachos. Created with skill and experience, Slope & Hatch's menu is filled with tasty, unique and generously portioned cuisine." -Anthony Bourdain (after he eventually eats there)
